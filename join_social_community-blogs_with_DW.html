JOIN SOCIAL COMMUNITY

BLOGS WITH IBM DEVELOPER WORKS
Hundreds of tutorials and courses, expert opinions on technologies and a place for co-operation with people interested in similar topics can be found IBM Developer Works Portal. Now you are going to use Developer Works to create your own blog and community.

Objectives
1. Inspect Rich Content of Developer Works
2. Create Your Own Community
3. Subscribe To Other People Communities


Exercises

1. Inspect Rich Content of Developer Works

TEXT


STEPS

a) Developer Works is a Portal for people interested in Software technologies. There are many communities where experts share the latest news from the field of development.


b) Using a browser connect to www.ibm.com/developerworks.


c) Sign up or register clicking an icon in the top right part of the screen.
Click Community. You should see a page with many already existing communities.




2. Create Your Own Community

TEXT


STEPS

a) Scroll down to the bottom of the screen. Select Communities in order to start your own community.


b) You should see a screen for initiate a community.




c) You are one step away from having your own blog. Click Start a Community.






3. Subscribe to Other People Communities

TEXT


STEPS

a) On the top right side of the screen, ou can find button that allows you to browse existing communities.


b) Click on Public Communities and search for a topic, for example Watson.


c) You can also use advanced search. It gives a lot of more search options.
