CREATE AND SHARE CONTENT

IBM BOX
Now you are going to use IBM BOX to share files on the cloud. You can invite others to see and edit selected files. Using IBM BOX, editing documents, speadsheets or presentations can be a real team work.

OBJECTIVES
1. Create your BOX account
2. Create your folders and files
3. Invite others to your folder


EXERCISES
1. Create your BOX account

Box is a cloud-based system for files manipulation. It gives you many interesting features from storing and sharing your documents to editing text, spreadsheets or presentations.


STEPS

a) Click on browser icon, connect to www.box.com and highlight the features from top menu.


b) Click on SIGN UP in the top-right corner of the screen.


c) Choose Invidual Plan that suits your needs and click SIGN UP.


d) Fill the form. Provide all necessary information.




e) Note that your initial folder has been created.






2. Create your folders and files

Using IBM BOX you can create various documents or a quick note. There are much more features that you can explore.


STEPS

a) Click on NEW and choose a type of the document you want to create.


b) Click on Quick Note to edit a simple text document.




c) Explore more option in the new menu.






3. Invite others to your folder

IBM Box allows you to share your folder with other people.


STEPS

a) Click on invite people.


b) Fill all necessary information and send invitations.
