CREATE AND SHARE CONTENT

IBM BOX
Now you are going to use IBM BOX to share files in the cloud. You can invite others to see and edit selected files. Using IBM BOX, editing documents, spreadsheets, or presentations becomes real teamwork.

Objectives
1. Create Your Box Account
2. Create Your Folders and Files
3. Invite Others to Your Folder


Exercises

1. Create Your BOX Account

You are going to create your BOX account and explore some of the features IBM BOX offers.


STEPS

a) In your browser, open www.box.com. Click the Features menu to explore IBM BOX features.


b) Click the SIGN UP button in the top-right corner of the page.


c) If prompted to choose the plan, choose the Individual Plan and click SIGN UP. Other wise continue to step d)


d) Fill in the form. Provide all the necessary information.




e) Note that your initial folder has been created.






2. Create Your Folders and Files

You are going to create a simple text document and see how IBM BOX allows you to share documents with others.


STEPS

a) Click the NEW button and choose the type of document you want to create.


b) Choose Box Note to edit a simple text document.




c) Explore more options in the new menu.






3. Invite Others to Your Folder

You will see how to share your documents and folders to enable teamwork.


STEPS

a) Click the Invite People button.


b) Provide all the necessary information and send the invitations.
